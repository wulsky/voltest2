La Coeur
Claymasks
For this assignment I had to make up a company that sells a product of my own choice.

I decided to make a beauty company called ”La Coeur” and the packaging would be for claymasks.
The packaging has a clean and spacious design where the focus lies within the country of which the claymask is from, since it’s of upmost importance for many claymask enthusiasts. This is backed up by the illustration in the bottom right corner and the iconic colors that are the culture near.
On the back there’s a simple tutorial illustrating how to apply the mask.
I designed multiple packages from different countries, and one travel package that contains five different claymasks.
